About Us

LILAC | Civic Engagement collaborates with community-based organizations to prepare students to become socially responsible leaders and citizens through purposeful action, reflection, and learning. We run a wide variety of programs that provide direct service and opportunities for students to reflect on that service inside and outside of the classroom.

Other Volunteer Opportunities

Civic Engagement maintains directories of local opportunities in non-profit organizations for students who wish to volunteer on their own, find summer internships, or find Praxis Independent Study field placements. Students who volunteer independently can receive 50% reimbursement for their travel expenses. Check out our Civic Engagement directories to find out about other volunteer opportunities!
